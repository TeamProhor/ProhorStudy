# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Students, researchers, and continuous learners who need an intelligent AI study companion to help them analyze materials, synthesize knowledge, and learn efficiently on any device.

## Product Purpose
A highly performant, mobile-first Study AI Web and Mobile App. It serves as a conversational study assistant, inspired by Claude, designed to optimize learning and information retrieval. Success is defined by fast task completion, smooth responsive interactions (via GSAP), and exceptional cross-device performance.

## Brand Personality
Warm, intelligent, editorial, and sophisticated. It feels like a trusted academic companion—capable but approachable, serious about knowledge but inviting to use.

## Anti-references
- Cool-toned, generic SaaS dashboards with heavy blue/cyan palettes.
- Cluttered, overwhelming, and complex academic portals.
- Sketchy, whimsical, or amateurish illustrations.

## Design Principles
- **Content is the interface:** Let the conversational and study material breathe. The UI should step back and frame the knowledge.
- **Mobile native, desktop expansive:** Interactions must feel fluid and native on mobile, and elegantly expand for desktop multi-tasking.
- **Warmth through restraint:** Use the editorial cream and coral palette to provide a literary, human feel rather than a cold robotic one.
- **Performance as a feature:** High optimization is critical; the tool must respond as fast as the user thinks.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- High contrast requirements met (especially body text on cream canvas).
- Fluid typography and responsive design ensuring legibility across all viewport sizes.
- Consideration for reduced motion (`prefers-reduced-motion`) given the use of GSAP animations.
